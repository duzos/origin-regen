## What is it?

An add-on that ties Doctor Who-style regeneration into the [Origins](https://modrinth.com/mod/origins) mod. Die, regenerate, and maybe come back as someone else entirely.

## With [Origins](https://modrinth.com/mod/origins)

- Adds the **Time Lord** origin, whose single power is **regeneration**.
- Adds the **Regeneration** power: **12** regenerations, and on your final one you're given a random origin.
- If you regenerate while you *aren't* a Time Lord, your body is unstable and your origin changes at random.

## With [Pehkui](https://modrinth.com/mod/pehkui)

- Randomly changes your size when you regenerate. (More planned.)

## Config

Every feature is toggleable and tweakable in-game via a config - this requires [Mod Menu](https://modrinth.com/mod/modmenu).
